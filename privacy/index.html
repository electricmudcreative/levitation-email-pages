Privacy & Data

Learn how LEVITATION uses your information and manage your email permissions.

What we collect

When you subscribe to our emails, we collect the following information to personalize your festival experience:

  • Email address — to send you lineup news, ticket alerts, and festival updates
  • Name — to personalize your emails (optional)
  • Newsletter preferences — which topics and genres interest you
  • Engagement data — opens, clicks, and purchase history to improve recommendations
  • Location — timezone info to send emails at the right time

How we use your data

Your data helps us deliver a better LEVITATION experience. We use it to:

  • Send relevant content — lineup news matched to your genre interests and preferences
  • Improve recommendations — surface artists, merch, and events you'll actually care about
  • Respect your preferences — only send what you've opted into
  • Measure performance — understand what content resonates so we can do more of it

We never sell your data to third parties. Your information stays within the LEVITATION ecosystem.

Your rights

You have full control over your data. Here's what you can do:

  • Update preferences — change which newsletters you receive at any time
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of any or all emails
  • Request your data — get a copy of the information we have about you
  • Delete your data — request that we remove your information from our systems
